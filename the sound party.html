The Sound Party

Description :


Sound Party is a fun and engaging event where participants' knowledge of music and soundtracks will be put to the test. In this competition, contestants will identify songs, music clips, or sound effects from various genres, movies, or games. The event will feature multiple rounds, such as rapid-fire identification, theme-based guessing, and even a "Guess the Sound" challenge where unique and unusual sounds will be played.
