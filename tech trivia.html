Tech Trivia

Description :


A dynamic event that brings together tech enthusiasts and innovators for a day of learning, collaboration, and fun. This event will feature engaging tech quizzes, interactive workshops, and cutting-edge demonstrations showcasing the latest advancements in AI, data science, web development, and more.


Rules :


How the Event Works:
    Round 1 (Finding jumbled/missing word):
  1. Everyone should find a technical word which is in jumbled or missing letter format.
    2. Round 2(Complete the Puzzle):
  2. Everyone should find the technical word which is in the riddle format and fill that in the given format of puzzle.
    3. Round 3(See, Hear, Speak):
  3. "One should identify the technical word which is in connection format and tell the technical definition of the word without saying the word to the other teammate and they should find the correct word".
Rules And Regulation:
  1. 3 Members.
  2. Max 3 Min 2 Members per team.
  3. 3 Mins for each round.
  4. Each round has 15 questions .
  5. No elimination or next level every team get a chance to play all rounds.
  6. These 3 rounds conducted in two station in the same venue simultaneously.
  7. Points are based on No.Of words identified.
  8. Winners are selected based on teams secured highest points.
  9. Round 2 will be conducted in question paper format.
Winning Criteria:
  • The team or Individuals who win the 2nd round will be crowned the winner.
